Introduction

Panel Data refers to datasets that contain observations of multiple entities (such as individuals, firms, countries) over multiple time periods. Unlike purely cross-sectional or time-series data, panel data captures both inter-individual (cross-sectional) and intra-individual (time-series) variations.
