Description

Right Ascension is a customizable on-rails team-based shooter. For each of your 5 planes, select what type of ship and what weapon they will have, as well as the formation that they will fly in. During the fight, you can switch between your ships and the AI will pilot the rest.

Complete traditional survival missions as well as escort ones. Players can also create their own challenges by creating a new level by selecting what enemy waves will appear.
