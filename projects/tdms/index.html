TDMS Inspector

by John Della Rosa

Local-only browser viewer for NI TDMS files.

Important notice: This tool is experimental and may contain parsing or calculation bugs. Do not rely on it as the sole source for safety-critical, regulatory, medical, legal, engineering, or production decisions. Always verify important results against trusted software or the original data source.

Sample file is available at https://www-personal.acfr.usyd.edu.au/zubizarreta/EDatabase.php
